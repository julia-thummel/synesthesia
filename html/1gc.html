Grapheme-Color Synesthesia

Grapheme-color synesthesia is an arbitrary but consistent association between a particular color and letter or number. Grapheme-color synesthesia can be projective or associative, depending on the person. Different people might associate different colors for each letter, however each person's associations will be consistent and unchanging.
